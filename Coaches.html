How to become coach:

To become coach, check this out or DM (Private message) GCVM on Lichess!

Coach manuall:

-As a coach, when you have a student you're coaching, please contact a leader about who that person is. This is needed for the ''rating'' system. The student will be asked by a leader to ''rate'' his or her coach.

-Furtherly, only start coaching someone when you're actually motivated to do it. If not, just politely decline their request to be your student. Once you accept someone as your student, you need to really do your best to let your student improve. If a student lets know that they are tired or they really need to go, stay polite and let them have their rest.

-As student, please listen to your coach' advice, and when he/she askes you to do a duel or a game, please do that, it's always asked with a reason.
