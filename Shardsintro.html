GCVM - Shards intro

About ''Shards'':

Shards are our official currency, for which you can ''buy'' various in-team items. You can buy things like passwords to training sectors, analys sessions with a high-rated player, promote your study, etc. More things getting added soon!

How to get shards?

-Claiming your daily free shards in the shop (resets at UTC)

-Solve the daily puzzle for 10 shards (resets daily, time depends)

-Weekly payment: Every member gets 25 free shards on saturday (time depends)

-For participating in in-team tournaments you get 1 Shard for every point gotten in that tournament. In some cases a (for example) x2 boost is active. If so, a mod says it in the tourney-chat!

-For having a ''job'', you can gain Shards. Depends on what kind of job, of course

-Inviting a player! Doesn't matter if it's a bot or not, for every player you invite (get's checked!) you get 25 Shards

-new ways to get Shards will get added soon!-
