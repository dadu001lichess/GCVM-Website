About us:

We are a Lichess team, who's main goal is to coach low-rated players to become better at chess! We also host (practise) tournaments and simuls, and we of course have fun! We also have features, like Member of The Month (MoTM), Co-leaders of the month, and more. This is our official website, and I hope you like it!
