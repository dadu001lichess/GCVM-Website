About us:

We are a Lichess team, who's main goal is to coach low rated players to become better at chess! We also host (practise) tournaments and simuls, and we ofcourse have fun! We also have features, like Member of The Month (MoTM), Co-leaders of the month, and more. This is our official website, and I hope you like it!

We also inspired people like GatoChess89 to also make a coaching team, with which we've created an "alliance", and we both help each other to make our teams better! He has had great ideas, and we even "stole" some of his, of course, with his knowing.
