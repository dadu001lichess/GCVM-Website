Training Drills

  • Tactics: 20 minutes of mixed tactics focusing on forks, pins and discovered attacks.
  • Endgames: Study one basic endgame (king and pawn, rook vs. pawn) and practice 10 positions.
  • Openings: Review one opening line and the common plans for both sides.

Weekly plan

  1. Mon — Tactics & 5 rapid games
  2. Tue — Endgames & review
  3. Wed — Opening study & annotated game
  4. Thu — Tactics & training game
  5. Fri — Review mistakes & coach feedback
